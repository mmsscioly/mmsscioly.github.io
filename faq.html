Faq

What is scioly?

Science Olympiad is a nationwide competition that spans over 23 different STEM-based events. Split into four sections - Life Science, Physics & Chemistry, Earth & Space, Technology & Inquiry -  it advances content past what is covered in a traditional science classroom through collaborative, team-based competition. More information can be found on the Science Olympiad official website: https://www.soinc.org/ 

MMS Science Olympiad is a feeder team to MHS Science Olympiad - a highly reputed team that placed first in the nation last year. MMS Scioly placed third in states last year.

how do i join the team?

We will be hosting tryouts in late August/early September - keep an eye out for upcoming dates! Join the Schoology Group to get access to any tryout information, interest forms, and practice material.

what is required of the team?

Science Olympians must come to meetings and must follow through on independent preparation as well.  All team members must attend mandatory meetings and inform the captains beforehand of absences.  Students should also plan to attend regionals if placed on the team, and be available for Invitationals and states.
