MMS SCIENCE OLYMPIAD

ABOUT US

Mason Middle School Science Olympiad is a group of 36 passionate and knowledgeable 7th and 8th graders that have demonstrated their scientific abilities on a tryout test and 4 peer elected high school captains. We are the 3rd Place team of the 2021 Ohio State Tournament. Throughout the year, our team works hours on end to prepare for competition and all that comes with it -- studying, building, practice tests from various different schools and universities -- using not only the scientific concepts learned over the course of the season, but also essential collaborative skills.

Contact us

Feel free to email us at mmsscienceolympiad@masonohioschools.com or mason.scienceolympiad@gmail.com for information about tryouts, sponsorships, dates, etc.
